Bowl of Cereal

If you like a quick and easy breakfast with a great flavor and nutritional value, you just found the perfect recipe!

  • Milk
  • Any Box or Bag of Cereal
  • One bottle vodka for extra punch
  1. Pour and drink a glass of Vodka
  2. Get a bowl and some milk and your cereal
  3. Pour the cereal in the bowl
  4. Pour the milk in the bowl
  5. Pour the vodka in the bowl
  6. Start eating! It's that simple!
