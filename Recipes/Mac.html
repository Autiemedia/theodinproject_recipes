Mac (Macaroni and Cheese)

Can you really go wrong with this?

  • Milk
  • Elbow noodles
  • cheese
  • salt
  • water
  • butter (1 stick)
  • One bottle vodka for extra punch
  1. Pour and drink a glass of Vodka
  2. Start boiling some water to put the mac noodles into
  3. Mix up your cheese and milk and vodka in a bowl
  4. after noodles are hot, strain them and put them back into the pan
  5. mix in your milk butter and cheese and vodka until it looks all cheesy
