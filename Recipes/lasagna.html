Lasagna

Lasagna in a white dish

This tasty ahh lasagna will be sure to make you want to eat at least something for dinner. And your friends won't think you're a stinky guy who can't cook.

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • 3 Cheese Dipping Sauce
  • 1 Pint Ragu Pasta Sauce
  • 8 Ounces Milk
  • One bottle vodka for extra punch
  1. Pour and drink a glass of Vodka
  2. Boil some water and get your noodles in there
  3. Mix your cheese and pasta sauce in a bowl
  4. Pour a cup or two of vodka in that mixture
  5. After the noodles are soft make layers with your mixture and the noodles
  6. Bake at 400 for 15 minutes
  7. Let rest for 4 minutes 24 seconds and then cut into squares for serving size!
